---
title: 'Get a License'
description: 'This guide will help you get a license for Lilith.'
---

To use Lilith you need to have a working license. To get one go to the #apply-here channel in
our [Discord](https://discord.gg/lilith) server and fill out the required fields.

<Warning>
	{' '}
	Please do not contact staff before waiting more than 24 hours. Doing so will
	result in a 14 days ban.
</Warning>
