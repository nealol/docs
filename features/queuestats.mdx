<Note>
	For Queuestats to work you need to have a valid API Key or a [paid Lilith plan](https://lilith.rip/pricing).
</Note>

Queuestats are Lilith's core feature, they allow you to see your opponent's statistics before the game starts.

<Info>
	{' '}
	Please note that if your opponent is nicked, Queuestats will attempt to denick
	the player but may fail.
</Info>

Supported gamemode are: Duels, Bed Wars, SkyWars, Wool Wars and Murder Mystery,_(but there's more to come!)_.

## Chats Stats

Chat Stats refers to a player's statistics as an in-game chat message.

#### Common stats

-   `[YAN ✪]` is the player's rank, it can be the default one from Hypixel or even a custom rank (like here). 
    Requires [Lilith Ultimate](https://lilith.rip/pricing).
	<br />
	You may turn custom ranks off with [Lilith Pro or higher](https://lilith.rip/pricing).
-   `Lvl` is the Hypixel network level of the player.
-   `W` is the amount of wins the player has.
-   `L` is the amount of losses the player has.
-   `W/L` is the win-loss ratio of the player.
-   `K/D` is the kill-death ratio of the player.
-   `[677✫]` is the gamemode level of the player.

<Tabs>
	<Tab title="Overall">

    	![](/images/overall-qs.png)

    	* `Status` is the status of the player. Green for online and red for offline.
    	* `AP` is the achievement points of the player.
    	* `Quests` is the number of quests the player has completed.
    	* `Karma` is the karma of the player.
    	* `First login` is the date of the player first login on Hypixel.

    </Tab>
    <Tab title="Duels">

    	![](/images/duels-qs.png)

    	* `WS` is the current winstreak of the player.
    	* `BWS` is the best winstreak of the player.

    </Tab>
    <Tab title="Bed Wars">

    	![](/images/bw-qs.png)

    	* `FK/D` is the final kill-death ratio of the player.

    </Tab>
    <Tab title="SkyWars">

    	![](/images/sw-qs.png)

    	* `T` is the total playtime of the player in SkyWars.
    	* `C` is the corruption chance of the player.
			<br />
			Corruption chance refers to the chance that a player adds to corrupt a game in SkyWars.

    </Tab>
    <Tab title="Wool Wars">

    	![](/images/ww-qs.png)

    	* `Class` is the Wool Wars class of the player.

    </Tab>
    <Tab title="Murder Mystery">

    	![](/images/mm-qs.png)

    	* `MK` is the amount of kills as murderer of the player.
    	* `DW` is the amount of wins as detective of the player.
    	* `S` refers to `Survivors`.
    	* `I` refers to `Infected`.

    </Tab>

</Tabs>

## Tab Stats

Tab Stats refers to a condensed player's statistics in the `List Player` menu _(AKA Tab list)_.

<Tabs>
	<Tab title="SkyWars">

    	![](/images/sw-tab.png)

    	* `[7☼]` is the player's SkyWars level.
    	* `0.65` is the player's SkyWars kill-death ratio.

    </Tab>
    <Tab title="Bed Wars">

    	![](/images/bw-tab.png)

    	* `[16✫]` is the player's Bed Wars level.
    	* `0.68` is the player's final kill-death ratio.

    </Tab>
    <Tab title="Wool Wars">

    	![](/images/ww-tab.png)

    	* `[6]` is the player's Wool Wars level.
    	* `112` is the player's Wool Wars wins.

    </Tab>
</Tabs>
