---
title: 'Stat Check'
description: "Lilith provides a powerful statchecking system for the most of Hypixel's core gamemodes."
---

Lilith comes with statchecking sytem available with `/sc <gamemodes> [target1] [target2]`.

-   Gamemodes are comma separated and can hold as many supported gamemodes as you can.
-   Targets are space separated values, they are not required. If you do not provide them, you will be the target. <br/>
    You can also use `@p` to check your party member's stats, or `@a` for the entire lobby you're in.

You can get an explanation of what each stats means by looking at <a href="/features/queuestats#common-stats">Queuestats</a>.

## Gamemodes

-   To check for __overall__ stats, gamemode = `overall`, `all`, `stats`, `general`, `main`, `server`.
