<Warning>
	{' '}
	You need to be in Lilith's [Discord](https://discord.gg/lilith) and have a valid
	[license](apply) for Lilith to work.
</Warning>

## Before you start

Make sure that:

-   You have a Microsoft account that owns Minecraft,
-   You downloaded the launcher from our [release page](https://github.com/lilithmod/launcher-v2/releases).

## Start up Lilith

<Steps>
	<Step title={'Start Lilith'}>
		Open the Launcher and click the "Launch" button,
	</Step>
	<Step title={'Wait...'}>
		Wait for Lilith to finish downloading,
	</Step>
	<Step title={'Verify your hardware'}>
		Lilith will prompt you to verify your hardware, therefore click the
		verify button,
	</Step>
	<Step title={'Login to your Discord account'}>
		Authorize Lilith to access your Discord account, and go back to the
		Launcher,
	</Step>
	<Step title={'Connect to Lilith'}>
		Launch Minecraft <control>1.8.9</control>{' '}
		<emphasis>(more supported versions may come with injection, no ETA)</emphasis>{' '}
		and connect to the IP
		<code>localhost</code>,
	</Step>
	<Step title={'Log in Minecraft'}>
		Login using your Microsoft credentials,
	</Step>
	<Step title={'Done!'}>
		You're done! Enjoy Lilith! You may need to tweak Lilith as you wish in
		the <a href="/features/">settings</a>.
	</Step>
</Steps>

You may watch an explanatory video [here](https://youtu.be/vAubOvnVacY).
